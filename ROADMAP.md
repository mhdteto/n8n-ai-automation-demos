# Roadmap

## Current version

v1.0 - Initial public version of the n8n AI Automation Demos library.

## Purpose

This project provides demo automation workflows using n8n, AI prompts and business logic for SMB and B2B use cases.

The goal is to help consultants, business owners and teams understand how AI can support practical workflows such as lead routing, email classification, CRM updates and content brief generation.

## Current scope

The current version includes:

- Inbound lead router workflow.
- AI email classifier workflow.
- Meeting summary to CRM workflow.
- Content brief generator workflow.
- Setup guide.
- Use case documentation.
- Workflow screenshots.
- Safety and data handling guidance.
- Sample test data.
- Human review guidelines.
