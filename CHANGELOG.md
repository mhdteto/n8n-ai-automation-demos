### Purpose

This first version provides practical demo workflows that show how AI can support simple business automation use cases.

The goal is to help consultants, SMBs and B2B teams understand how to structure automation logic safely and clearly.

### Notes

These workflows are demos.

They are not production-ready systems.

Before using any workflow with real business data, review credentials, permissions, privacy requirements, error handling, human approval steps and compliance constraints.
