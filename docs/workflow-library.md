# Workflow Library

This document explains the demo workflows included in this repository.

---

## 1. Inbound Lead Router

### Goal

Classify inbound leads and route them to the right owner or team.

### Example input

- Name.
- Company.
- Email.
- Message.
- Source.
- Company size.
- Service interest.

### Example output

- Lead category.
- Urgency level.
- Suggested owner.
- Suggested next step.
- Internal notification.

### Business value

This workflow helps reduce manual lead sorting and improves response speed.

### Human review needed

Yes.

A person should review the classification before taking commercial action.

---

## 2. AI Email Classifier

### Goal

Classify inbound emails by intent and priority.

### Example categories

- Sales inquiry.
- Support request.
- Partnership.
- Billing.
- Spam or irrelevant.
- Urgent customer issue.

### Example output

- Intent.
- Priority.
- Suggested label.
- Suggested owner.
- Summary.
- Recommended next action.

### Business value

This workflow helps teams triage emails faster and avoid missing important requests.

### Human review needed

Yes.

AI classification can be wrong, especially for ambiguous messages.

---

## 3. Meeting Summary to CRM

### Goal

Turn meeting notes into structured CRM updates.

### Example input

- Meeting transcript.
- Notes.
- Account name.
- Contact name.
- Opportunity context.

### Example output

- Meeting summary.
- Pain points.
- Decision criteria.
- Next steps.
- Risks.
- Follow-up tasks.
- CRM-ready notes.

### Business value

This workflow helps sales teams keep CRM records cleaner and more consistent.

### Human review needed

Yes.

A sales owner should review the CRM update before saving it.

---

## 4. Content Brief Generator

### Goal

Generate structured content briefs for SEO, AEO and GEO workflows.

### Example input

- Topic.
- Target keyword.
- Target audience.
- Business context.
- Search intent.
- Competitors.

### Example output

- Suggested title.
- Search intent.
- Outline.
- Questions to answer.
- Entity terms.
- Internal link suggestions.
- CTA suggestions.

### Business value

This workflow helps marketing teams produce more structured content briefs faster.

### Human review needed

Yes.

A content strategist should review accuracy, positioning and brand alignment.
