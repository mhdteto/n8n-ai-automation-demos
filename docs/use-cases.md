# Automation Use Cases

## 1. Inbound lead router

Classifies incoming leads and routes them to the right sales owner.

## 2. AI email classifier

Reads inbound emails and classifies them by intent.

## 3. Meeting summary to CRM

Transforms meeting notes into structured CRM updates.

## 4. Content brief generator

Creates SEO/AEO/GEO content briefs from target keywords and business context.

## Prioritization criteria

| Criterion | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Repetition | The task happens often enough to automate. |
| Clear input | The workflow can start from structured or semi-structured data. |
| Low risk | Mistakes are reviewable before customer impact. |
| Business value | The workflow saves time or improves conversion quality. |
| Human review | Sensitive outputs can be approved before use. |
